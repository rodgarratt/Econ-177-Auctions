How Proof of Work Functions

SHA-256 is a one-way function — you cannot reverse-engineer what input produces a specific output. The only way to find a hash with leading zeros is trial and error.

Bitcoin miners do this trillions of times per second, competing to find a nonce that satisfies the current difficulty target. The winner gets to add the next block and earn the block reward.
